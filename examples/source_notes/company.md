# Northwind Robotics

Northwind Robotics builds field-service robots for regional utilities and warehouse operators.

Revenue grew 42 percent year over year after the company expanded from pilot deployments into multi-site contracts.

Gross margin improved from 38 percent to 46 percent as the company standardized its actuator and sensor assemblies.

The main risk is customer concentration: three customers represent 61 percent of annual recurring revenue.
